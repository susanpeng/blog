Technical Writer
I am a technical writer with four years of technical writing experience and more than ten years of work experience in the IT field. My strong IT background and extensive experiences make me an excellent technical writer who understands technology thoroughly and can translate complex technical facts into easily understood documents for technical and non-technical audiences. I have written many technical documents, including user manuals, online help, installation guidelines, and documents in other fields.

System Analyst/Developer
I have over ten years of experience with system analysis, development, and database administration. I have worked on many projects, including financial information management applications, bus routing information management web applications, etc. My skillset: Windows/Mac/Linux operating system; Oracle, MySQL, and MS SQL Server; Java, C++, SQL, HTML, CSS, Python and R.

Video/Audio Producing
I have three years of experience with audio recording, video shooting, and audio/video editing. I enjoy creating video/audio products and have completed many video/audio projects.

Teaching
